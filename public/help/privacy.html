Privacy Policy – Swiss QR Invoice

Last updated: July 2026 · Entity: Martin Pöhl, Switzerland

Summary

Swiss QR Invoice is a client-side monday.com app. All processing happens in your browser inside monday.com. The app operates no own servers, uses no third-party services, no analytics and no cookies.

What data the app processes

  • Board data (customer name, address, amount, reference) is read through the official monday.com API using your existing monday session, solely to generate the QR bill PDF you request. It is processed in your browser only and never transmitted elsewhere.
  • Your payee details (company name, address, IBAN/QR-IBAN) and your column mapping are stored in monday.com's app storage (scoped to your app instance, hosted by monday.com on its certified infrastructure). They never leave the monday.com platform.
  • Generated PDFs are created in your browser and downloaded directly to your device. They are not uploaded or stored anywhere.

What the app does NOT do

  • No transfer of any data to servers of the developer or third parties.
  • No tracking, no analytics, no advertising, no cookies.
  • No storage of board content outside your browser session.

Permissions (OAuth scopes)

The app requests the boards:read scope only, to read the columns and items of the board on which you use the view.

Data retention and deletion

Settings stored in monday app storage are removed in accordance with monday.com's platform policies when the app is uninstalled, and in any case within 10 days of an uninstall or upon request to the support address below.
