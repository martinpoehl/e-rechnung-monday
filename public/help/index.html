Swiss QR Invoice – How to use

Create compliant Swiss QR bills (QR-Rechnung) directly from your monday.com boards. Everything runs in your browser – no data leaves monday.

Prerequisites

  • A monday.com board that contains your customer data (name, street, postal code, city) and an amount column.
  • Your own IBAN or QR-IBAN. With a QR-IBAN, each invoice also needs a QR reference (QRR) column.

Installation

  1. Install the app from the monday.com marketplace.
  2. Open your board and click “+” next to the view tabs → Apps → Swiss QR Invoice.

First use – 3 steps

1 · Your details (payee)
Enter your company name, address and IBAN/QR-IBAN once. Choose the PDF format (full A4 page or payment part only) and the invoice language (DE/FR/IT/EN). Everything is saved automatically.
2 · Customer data from the board
Tell the app which board columns contain the customer data. Matching columns are suggested automatically – just review and complete the dropdowns marked with *.
3 · Generate
Click “Create PDF” next to any item. The QR bill is validated (IBAN checksum, reference rules, field lengths) and downloaded as a PDF. Scan the QR code with any Swiss banking app to verify.

Notes

  • QR-IBAN vs. IBAN: a QR-IBAN requires a valid QR reference; a regular IBAN must not have one. The app checks this and shows a clear message.
  • Privacy: the PDF is generated entirely in your browser. Customer and payment data never reach any third-party server.
  • Languages: the app interface follows your monday language (DE/FR/IT/EN); the invoice language is set per board view in step 1.

Anleitung (Deutsch)

Erste Schritte

  1. App aus dem monday.com-Marketplace installieren.
  2. Board öffnen → «+» neben den View-Tabs → Apps → Swiss QR Invoice.
  3. Schritt 1: Absenderdaten (Firma, Adresse, IBAN/QR-IBAN) einmal erfassen; PDF-Format und Rechnungssprache wählen.
  4. Schritt 2: Board-Spalten den Rechnungsfeldern zuordnen – Vorschläge werden automatisch gesetzt.
  5. Schritt 3: Beim gewünschten Eintrag auf «PDF erstellen» klicken – die QR-Rechnung wird geprüft und heruntergeladen.

Hinweis: Eine QR-IBAN braucht eine gültige QR-Referenz; eine normale IBAN darf keine haben. Die App prüft das automatisch.
